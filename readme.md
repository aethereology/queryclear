# queryclear

**We make your website easier for search engines and AI answer engines to
understand, trust, and recommend.**

queryclear is a **GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) / AI Search Optimization**
service — we optimize websites so search engines and AI answer engines like
ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Gemini, Google AI Overviews, and Bing Copilot can
better crawl, understand, summarize, trust, and cite a business.

queryclear is the first product of **Aethelo**, under **SparkCreatives Inc.**

> **Status:** In development. This repo currently contains strategy and product
> docs; the site and sample audit are being built (see `roadmap.md`).

## What we do

- **AI Search Optimization Audit** — a scored review of your site's AI-search
  readiness with a prioritized fix list, including live AI-visibility tests.
- **GEO Website Upgrade** — we implement the fixes (schema, `llms.txt`, sitemap,
  metadata, FAQs, structure, crawlability) on your existing site.
- **AI Search Website Build** — a new, AI-search-ready website.

## What we promise

Better technical readiness, clearer content, valid structured data,
crawlability, and honest measurement. We do **not** promise guaranteed rankings
or guaranteed AI citations, and we never invent business details, reviews, or
testimonials.
