## Day 5 - Tuesday, June 23

Theme: schema education.

### LinkedIn

"Schema" sounds technical.

Here is the plain-English version:

Schema is a label.

It tells search systems:

This is a business.
This is the business name.
This is the service.
This is the location.
This is the founder.
This is the FAQ.
This is the page topic.

Without it, search engines and AI systems can still read the page.

But they have to infer more.

For a local service business, that inference can get messy fast:

- multiple service areas
- multiple practitioners
- inconsistent names
- unclear categories
- outdated directory listings
- thin homepage copy

Schema will not magically make you rank.

It will not force ChatGPT to recommend you.

But it reduces ambiguity.

And in AI search, reducing ambiguity is the whole game.
