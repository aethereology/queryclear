# queryclear 5-Day Zero-Follower Marketing Kickstart

Launch window: Monday, June 22, 2026 through Friday, June 26, 2026.

Goal: generate the first free audits and sales conversations. Do not optimize for follower count.

Primary wedge: med spas. Dental and custom home builders are backup verticals if med spa volume or fit is weak.

Primary CTA: `https://www.queryclear.com/free-audit`

## Positioning

queryclear is modern SEO for the AI search era. We help service businesses make their websites clearer, crawlable, structured, and easier for Google, ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, Gemini, and other AI search experiences to understand.

Never promise rankings, traffic, or AI citations. The honest claim is readiness: clearer pages, stronger structure, better local/service clarity, useful FAQs, schema where appropriate, and a better conversion path.

## Daily Operating Rhythm

Every day during the sprint:

- Send 20 targeted emails to med spa owners or marketing contacts.
- Post once on LinkedIn from the founder account; repost from queryclear.
- Post one short X post or thread.
- Publish one Instagram post, carousel, or story set.
- Publish the same short video to TikTok and YouTube Shorts.
- Post on the Facebook Page; engage in groups only where self-promotion is allowed.
- Leave 5 useful Reddit comments with no link unless directly relevant and allowed.
- DM or reply to anyone who engages.
- Record results in `launch-tracker.csv`.

## Compliance Guardrails

- Cold email must include honest sender info, a non-deceptive subject, a physical mailing address, and an opt-out line.
- Reddit is value-first. Do not repeat promotional links across communities.
- Any named teardown needs permission. Without permission, anonymize the business.
- Do not say "guaranteed ranking", "guaranteed citation", "get into ChatGPT", or "AI ranking score".
